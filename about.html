About F-square

Craft, culture, and cloth — since the first stitch

F-square began in Port Harcourt with a single loom and a belief that African fashion deserves the same reverence as any global luxury house. Today, every piece we ship still passes through the same artisan hands.

Our story

F-square was founded on a simple conviction: that the fabrics woven into Nigerian life — Ankara, Adire, Aso-oke, Kente — carry stories worth preserving and worth wearing forward. We work directly with weavers and dye artisans across Rivers, Oyo, and Abeokuta, paying fair prices for their craft and bringing their work to a wider audience.

Every garment is cut and finished in our Port Harcourt atelier by resident tailors, many of whom trained under the same masters who taught their parents. Nothing about what we do is mass-produced — from the dye pit to the final stitch, a person made this by hand.
